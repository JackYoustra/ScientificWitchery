Jack Youstra is the founder and lead developer of NanoFlick, a mobile app that allows users to create and share short videos. Jack has been developing iOS apps since 2012.

He got his CS degree from UT Austin and did some work at Apple and Citadel before working on NanoFlick full time.

Techwise, he likes iOS development, browsers, compilers, writing mods for Paradox games, and random deep learning things. He also likes Star Wars. A lot.
